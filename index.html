"All Peaceful Paths to God are Equally Valid"

Our Interfaith Ministry recognizes that your marriage joins you both together as a couple within the community of your families and friends. Our ceremonies honor your wishes to be inclusive of all your loved ones in celebration of your Wedding



Dr. Rich Goldman will work directly with you in creating and designing your wedding ceremony. Every ceremony is uniquely designed to meet your needs, the needs of your loved ones and your community.

Rev. Rich will help you understand the elements of a marriage ceremony and assist you in customizing every aspect of it. Rev. Rich is a member of both the Association of Interfaith Ministers and the Interfaith Ministries International. He and his wife, Patty, are Licensed Professionals and National Certified Counselors focusing on Marriage, Family and Wellness Counseling. Based in the Volusia-Flagler area of Florida, he enjoys officiating at weddings anywhere in the U.S. or abroad, with Patty serving as the ceremony coordinator.

Marriage rites approved in Florida.
