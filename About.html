History  ::  Mission  ::  Professional Staff

The belief that all peaceful paths to God are equally valid is inherent in the philosophy that drives the activities of The ACT II Interfaith Center. The mission of The ACT II Interfaith Center is to help people of all faiths and cultures make and implement positive choices that produce positive outcomes in their lives and in the lives of others, so that they may thrive spiritually, emotionally, and physically. The original mission of ACT II was to coach and counsel those who were living with cancer. Its mission then expanded to helping individuals, couples and families facing other serious and life-threatening illnesses. Marriage and family relationships became a focus for ACT II as the stresses of illness and disease pressed these critical areas of living and thriving. Coaching pre-marital couples, married couples, stepfamilies and interfaith families followed course naturally after Rev. Rich became ordained.new site
